How to read the map
Area fills and point markers are colored by average sentiment score. For accessibility, each sentiment band is also explained with a star rating and a short text label.
⭐ 1 star · dark red · very negativeScore ≤ -0.35Strongly negative average sentiment.
⭐⭐ 2 stars · orange · negative-0.35 < score ≤ -0.10Moderately negative average sentiment.
⭐⭐⭐ 3 stars · yellow · mixed / neutral-0.10 < score ≤ 0.10Mixed or near-neutral average sentiment.
⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4 stars · light green · positive0.10 < score ≤ 0.35Moderately positive average sentiment.
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 5 stars · dark green · very positiveScore > 0.35Strongly positive average sentiment.
